Featured Story

From COVID Crisis to National Movement

When COVID-19 struck India in 2020, a group of young students decided they couldn't just watch. Starting with food distribution in their local community, NayePankh Foundation has now grown into a national movement spanning 28 states, training 30,000+ interns, and impacting over 2 lakh lives through education, healthcare, and environmental campaigns.

What started as a small act of kindness has become proof that young people can change the world — one community at a time.
