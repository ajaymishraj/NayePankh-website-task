Our Story

About NayePankh Foundation

Born during India's toughest days, we've grown into a movement of 30,000+ young changemakers dedicated to building a better tomorrow.

🎯

Our Mission

To empower underprivileged communities by providing access to quality education, healthcare, and opportunities, while nurturing the next generation of social leaders through our internship programs.

🔭

Our Vision

A world where every individual, regardless of their background, has the wings ("Pankh") to fly — access to education, dignity, health, and the opportunity to reach their fullest potential.

Our Journey

From COVID Crisis to National Impact

Every milestone in our journey represents thousands of lives touched and communities empowered.

🌱 2020

The Beginning

Founded during the COVID-19 pandemic to help communities in need. Started with food distribution and essential supplies.

📚 2021

Education Initiative

Launched free education programs and mentorship for underprivileged children. Started our first internship cohort with 500 students.

🚀 2022

Rapid Growth

Expanded to 15+ states. 10,000+ interns joined. Registered under NITI Aayog, obtained 12A and 80G certification.

🏆 2023

National Recognition

Featured in multiple newspapers. Crossed 20,000 interns. Launched women empowerment and environmental conservation programs.

🌟 2024

30K+ Strong

30,000+ interns trained, 2 lakh+ lives impacted, 500+ campaigns run across 28 states. The movement continues to grow!
